The Company

May-Mac Projects was established in 2015 in response to the need to fullfill the growing demand for professional security services in the country. The company is 100% owned by Black South Africans and it subscribes to the principles of affirmation and diversity. This Company is proponent of broad based black Economic Empowerment, which believes that the Socio-economic imbalances of the apartheid past require systematic redressal methodology so that the route towards closing the gap is to create an atmosphere wherein blacks are part of the economic mainstream.

Co-founder: Mzwonke Mayekiso

Who we are

May-Mac Projects is a specialized, professional and innovative service provider, whose sole function is providing Security guards, Landscapers, Cleaners, Hygiene products, etc and training services nationally. The company offers highly specialized security with its human resources coming from Previously disadvantaged trained groups. It offers its services to industrial, commercial and domestic markets.
